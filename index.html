How we work

Schedule a Pickup

You can schedule a pickup of your scrap materials by contacting us through our website, social media handles or by phone. Our team will get in touch with you to confirm the details of the pickup. We will gather important details from you, such as your name, address, phone number, and the type and quantity of scrap materials you have available for pickup. This information helps us determine the best way to handle your materials and ensure a smooth pickup process. We are flexible and can work around your schedule to make the pickup as easy and hassle-free as possible.

On-time Pickup

On the scheduled day of pickup, our team will arrive at your location ready to collect your scrap materials. Our team is fully equipped with the necessary tools and safety equipments to collect the scrap.

Payment

After the scrap materials are collected, we will weigh them and pay you according to the agreed-upon rate. We believe in fair and transparent pricing, and we will always pay you a fair price for your scrap materials.
